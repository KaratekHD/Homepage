Away from Keyboard mode

You can mark yourself as afk, just by typing /afk or brb. As soon as you write any other message, you will automatically get marked as available. While you are afk, people who mention you (@Karatek_HD) will get notified about you beeing afk. You can even set a reason for you being afk by using /afk <reason>
